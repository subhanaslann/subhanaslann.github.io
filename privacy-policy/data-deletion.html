Alarm & Equalizer - Data Deletion Request

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This page explains how you can delete your data associated with the Alarm & Equalizer app and how to request deletion of any data held by third-party services.

1. Delete Data Directly from the App

You can delete all locally stored data directly within the app:

  1. Open Alarm & Equalizer
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete All Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

This will permanently remove all locally stored data including alarm configurations, preferences, consent settings, and purchase status from your device.

2. Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Alarm & Equalizer from your device will automatically remove all locally stored data, including:

  • Alarm configurations (times, labels, repeat schedules)
  • Custom alarm sound selections
  • App preferences (theme, language)
  • Consent and purchase status

3. Request Deletion of Third-Party Data

Some data may be held by third-party services used by the app. To request deletion of this data, send an email to tektechgroup.info@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Email template:
To: tektechgroup.info@gmail.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request

I would like to request the deletion of all my data associated with the Alarm & Equalizer app. My device information: [your device model and Android version].

We will process your request within 30 days and delete or anonymize all identifiable data held by third-party services on your behalf.

4. Data Types and Retention

Data Type Storage Deletion Method Retention After Deletion
Alarm settings, preferences Device only In-app or uninstall Deleted immediately
Analytics data Firebase Analytics Email request Up to 60 days
Crash reports Firebase Crashlytics Email request Up to 90 days
Advertising data Google AdMob Reset Advertising ID in device settings Per Google's policy
Purchase history Google Play Managed by Google Play Per Google's policy
Note: Purchase history is managed by Google Play and cannot be deleted by us. You can manage your Google Play purchase history through your Google account settings.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding data deletion, please contact us:

  • Email: tektechgroup.info@gmail.com
  • Developer: TekTech Group

Alarm & Equalizer - Veri Silme Talebi

Son guncelleme: 9 Subat 2026

Bu sayfa, Alarm & Equalizer uygulamasiyla iliskili verilerinizi nasil silebileceginizi ve ucuncu taraf hizmetleri tarafindan tutulan verilerin silinmesini nasil talep edebileceginizi aciklar.

1. Verileri Dogrudan Uygulamadan Silin

Yerel olarak saklanan tum verileri dogrudan uygulama icinden silebilirsiniz:

  1. Alarm & Equalizer uygulamasini acin
  2. Ayarlar bolumune gidin
  3. "Tum Verileri Sil" secenegine dokunun
  4. Silme islemini onaylayin

Bu islem, alarm yapilandirmalari, tercihler, onay ayarlari ve satin alma durumu dahil olmak uzere cihazinizda yerel olarak saklanan tum verileri kalici olarak silecektir.

2. Uygulamayi Kaldirin

Alarm & Equalizer'i cihazinizdan kaldirmak, yerel olarak saklanan tum verileri otomatik olarak siler:

  • Alarm yapilandirmalari (saatler, etiketler, tekrar programlari)
  • Ozel alarm ses secimleri
  • Uygulama tercihleri (tema, dil)
  • Onay ve satin alma durumu

3. Ucuncu Taraf Verilerinin Silinmesini Talep Edin

Bazi veriler, uygulama tarafindan kullanilan ucuncu taraf hizmetleri tarafindan tutulabilir. Bu verilerin silinmesini talep etmek icin tektechgroup.info@gmail.com adresine "Veri Silme Talebi" konu basligiyla bir e-posta gonderin.

E-posta sablonu:
Kime: tektechgroup.info@gmail.com
Konu: Veri Silme Talebi

Alarm & Equalizer uygulamasiyla iliskili tum verilerimin silinmesini talep ediyorum. Cihaz bilgilerim: [cihaz modeliniz ve Android surumunuz].

Talebinizi 30 gun icinde isleyecek ve ucuncu taraf hizmetleri tarafindan tutulan tum tanimlanabilir verileri sizin adiniza silecek veya anonimlestirecegiz.
