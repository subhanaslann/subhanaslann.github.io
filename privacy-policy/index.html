Alarm & Equalizer - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Alarm & Equalizer ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by TekTech Group, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (package name: com.alarmy.intervalclock).

By using Alarm & Equalizer, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Alarm configurations (times, labels, repeat schedules, interval settings)
  • Custom alarm sound selections
  • App preferences (theme, language, notification settings)
  • Consent preferences
  • Purchase status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the alarm functionality
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Display advertisements (with your consent)
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Manage remote configuration for app features

3. Advertisements

Alarm & Equalizer displays advertisements through Google AdMob. The types of ads include:

  • Banner ads
  • Interstitial (full-screen) ads
  • Rewarded video ads

You can choose whether to allow personalized ads during the initial consent dialog or in the app's Settings. If you decline personalized ads, you will still see non-personalized advertisements.

You may remove all advertisements by purchasing the premium version through in-app purchase.

For more information about how Google uses data from ads, please visit: Google Advertising Policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Data is shared only with the third-party service providers listed in Section 4, solely for the purposes described in this policy. Specifically:

  • Google (Firebase & AdMob): Analytics data, crash reports, and ad-related data are processed by Google under their privacy policies.
  • Google Play: Purchase transaction data is processed by Google Play for billing purposes.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or delete data manually.
  • Firebase Analytics: Data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies (up to 14 months).
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash data is retained for 90 days.
  • AdMob: Data retention follows Google's advertising data policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

8.1 Consent Management

  • On first launch, you are presented with a consent dialog where you can choose to enable or disable personalized ads and analytics data collection.
  • You can change your consent preferences at any time from the Settings screen within the app.

8.2 Data Deletion

  • You can delete all locally stored data through the app's Settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device.
  • To request deletion of data held by Firebase, contact us at the email address below.

8.3 Opt-Out

  • You can opt out of analytics data collection through the consent settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through the consent settings.
  • You can reset your Google Advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your device's Settings.

9. GDPR Compliance (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request information about the data we process.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request restriction of data processing.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to data processing based on legitimate interests.

Legal basis for processing: We process your data based on your explicit consent, which you can withdraw at any time through the app's Settings.

10. KVKK Compliance (Turkey)

If you are located in Turkey, your data is processed in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK). You have the right to:

  • Learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • Request information about data processing
  • Learn the purpose of data processing and whether it is used accordingly
  • Know the third parties to whom your data is transferred
  • Request correction of incomplete or incorrect data
  • Request deletion or destruction of your data
  • Object to automated processing that produces a result against you
  • Claim compensation for damages arising from unlawful processing

11. Children's Privacy

Alarm & Equalizer is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

12. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted to third-party services (Firebase, AdMob) is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL.
  • Local data is stored in the app's private storage area, protected by the Android operating system's sandboxing mechanism.
  • We do not store any personal data on our own servers.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Increment the consent version in the app, which will prompt you to review and accept the updated policy.
